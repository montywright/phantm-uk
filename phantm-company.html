About Phantm

Trusted data for better packaging decisions.

Phantm helps businesses collect, refine and structure the packaging data they need to reduce risk, improve reporting and make better decisions across cost, compliance and design.

We work with brands, retailers and manufacturers across the UK navigating EPR, Plastic Packaging Tax, Digital Product Passport readiness and evolving market obligations.

Our Mission

A world with no packaging waste.

Our vision is bold: a world with no packaging waste. Our mission is simple: empowering better packaging decisions with trusted data to prevent waste at the source.

We believe that better data leads to better decisions — and better decisions lead to less waste, less cost and less regulatory risk for everyone in the supply chain.

How we work

What we believe about packaging data.

Every principle we work by comes back to the same idea — that better data, properly structured and reused, leads to better outcomes for businesses and the planet.

01

Capture once, repurpose forever

Data collected well shouldn't need to be collected again. We build reusable assets, not one-off reports.

02

Trust requires evidence

Every data point links to its source. Validation status is visible. Nothing is assumed — everything is traceable.

03

Regulation as advantage

Businesses that build data infrastructure now will navigate EPR, DPP and future obligations faster and at lower cost.

04

Suppliers are part of the system

The best packaging data comes from suppliers confirming what they know — not businesses guessing from the outside.

05

Data compounds over time

The first report is the hardest. Each validation event and supplier response makes the next cycle faster and more reliable.

06

Your data stays yours

We don't pool raw client data. You own what you build. Export it, integrate it, take it with you — no lock-in.
